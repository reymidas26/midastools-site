## Pro Tips for Educational Videos

1. **Pace matters** — educational content should be slightly slower than marketing content
2. **Text on screen** — always include text for accessibility and sound-off viewing
3. **Chunk information** — one concept per clip, 3-5 key points per video maximum
4. **Visual hierarchy** — use size, color, and position to guide the viewer's eye
5. **Repetition** — show key concepts twice in different ways for retention
6. **Progress indicators** — add step numbers, progress bars, or module markers
7. **Closed captions** — design videos that work without sound (most social viewing is muted)
