## Pro Tips for Viral & Trending Videos

1. **Trends move FAST** — what's viral this week may be dead next week. Adapt the styles to current trends
2. **First 1 second = everything** — if the opening frame doesn't hook, viewers scroll past
3. **Sound matters** — design for trending audio (even if the video is AI-generated, the edit matters)
4. **Authenticity wins** — UGC-style and slightly imperfect content outperforms polished corporate videos
5. **Controversy drives comments** — "This or That" and opinion-based content gets more engagement
6. **Save-worthy content** = educational + aesthetic. Make people want to bookmark it
7. **Share-worthy content** = relatable + funny. "This is SO me" drives shares
8. **Aspect ratios**: 9:16 for TikTok/Reels (always), 1:1 for feed posts, 16:9 only for YouTube
