## Company Culture & Brand

### 1. Company Overview Video
```

Scene 5 (15-20s): Team photo style — everyone together, smiling. Logo fade-in. Natural lighting. Warm, authentic feel. 16:9 horizontal.

## Pro Tips for Business & Corporate Videos

1. **Professionalism ≠ boring** — inject warmth, humanity, and genuine emotion
2. **Faces build trust** — always include real (or realistic) people in corporate videos
3. **Keep it concise** — busy professionals won't watch beyond 30 seconds unless hooked
4. **Subtitles/captions** are essential — many corporate videos are watched on mute (office, LinkedIn)
5. **Brand consistency** — use your exact brand colors, fonts, and tone in every video
6. **Call to action** — every video should have a clear next step for the viewer
7. **Quality signals trust** — investing in polished visuals signals a professional organization
