## Product Demo Videos

### 1. Hero Product Reveal
```
A cinematic 8-second video of [YOUR PRODUCT] on a [SURFACE — e.g., "matte black table" / "white marble pedestal"]. The scene starts in darkness. A single spotlight slowly fades in from above, revealing the product with a warm golden glow. The camera orbits slowly around the product (180 degrees). Reflections on the surface beneath. Studio quality. Shallow depth of field. 16:9 horizontal.
```

### 2. Product in Use — Lifestyle
```
A 10-second lifestyle video showing [PERSON DESCRIPTION] using [YOUR PRODUCT] in [SETTING — e.g., "a bright, modern kitchen" / "a cozy home office"]. Natural morning light streaming through windows. The person interacts with the product naturally — [SPECIFIC ACTION]. The camera follows the action with a smooth tracking shot. Warm, aspirational color grading. 16:9 horizontal.
```

### 3. 360-Degree Product Spin
```
A smooth 6-second video of [YOUR PRODUCT] rotating 360 degrees on a clean white background. The product is centered and well-lit with soft studio lighting from three angles. The rotation is smooth and constant. No shadows or distracting elements. Perfect for product pages. Slightly elevated camera angle (30 degrees from horizontal). 1:1 square format.
```

### 4. Feature Highlight Close-Up
```
A series of 3-second close-up clips of [YOUR PRODUCT]. Clip 1: extreme close-up of [FEATURE 1 — e.g., "the texture of the fabric"]. Clip 2: close-up of [FEATURE 2 — e.g., "the stitching detail"]. Clip 3: close-up of [FEATURE 3 — e.g., "the brand logo embossed"]. Each clip has a slow pull-back revealing the detail. Macro lens style. Studio lighting. 16:9 horizontal.
```

### 5. Size & Scale Comparison
```
A 5-second video showing [YOUR PRODUCT] next to common objects for size reference — [COMPARISON OBJECTS — e.g., "a coffee mug, a hand, a laptop"]. The camera starts on the comparison object, then pans to reveal the product beside it. Clean white background. Even lighting. Informational and clear. 16:9 horizontal.
```

## Advertisement Videos

### 6. Problem-Solution Ad (15 seconds)
```
A 15-second ad-style video. Scene 1 (0-5s): [PROBLEM SCENE — e.g., "a frustrated person staring at a messy desk, head in hands. Grey, desaturated color grading. Dim lighting"]. Scene 2 (5-8s): transition with a bright flash or smooth wipe. Scene 3 (8-15s): [SOLUTION SCENE — e.g., "the same person smiling at a clean desk with YOUR PRODUCT, bright and warm lighting"]. The contrast between scenes is dramatic. 16:9 horizontal.
```

### 7. Social Proof Ad
```
A 10-second video showing [MULTIPLE PEOPLE — e.g., "5 different people in different settings"] each using [YOUR PRODUCT] and reacting positively — smiling, nodding, giving thumbs up. Quick cuts between each person (2 seconds each). Diverse settings (home, office, café, outdoors, gym). Energetic pacing. Warm, happy color grading. 9:16 vertical for paid social.

### 8. Urgency/Scarcity Ad

Quick cuts. Dynamic camera movement. Red accent lighting for urgency. Bold, high-contrast color grading. Designed for retargeting ads. 9:16 vertical.
```

### 9. Comparison Ad
```

Right side labeled "With [YOUR PRODUCT]": [THRIVING SCENE — e.g., "the same task done effortlessly, fast, with a smile"]. Both sides play simultaneously. The "with" side has brighter, warmer lighting. 16:9 horizontal.

## Pro Tips for Product & Marketing Videos

1. **Lead with the product** — show your product within the first 2 seconds
2. **Lighting is everything** — specify "studio lighting", "golden hour", or "natural window light"
3. **Aspect ratios**: 16:9 for YouTube/websites, 9:16 for TikTok/Reels/Stories, 1:1 for feeds
4. **Keep ads short** — 6-15 seconds for paid social, 15-30 for YouTube pre-roll
5. **UGC style outperforms polished** — for social ads, request "authentic, phone-camera feel"
6. **Always specify camera movement** — static, pan, zoom, orbit, tracking, dolly
7. **Color psychology**: warm = trustworthy, cool = professional, bright = energetic
