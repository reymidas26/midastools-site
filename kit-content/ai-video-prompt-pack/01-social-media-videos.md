## TikTok & Reels (Vertical 9:16)

### 1. Product Showcase Reel
```
A smooth, cinematic 5-second video of [YOUR PRODUCT] rotating slowly on a clean white pedestal. Soft studio lighting from the left with a gentle shadow. The camera starts close-up on a detail, then slowly pulls back to reveal the full product. Shallow depth of field. Professional product photography style. Vertical 9:16 format. Subtle warm color grading.
```

### 2. Satisfying Process Video
```
A top-down overhead shot of hands [YOUR PROCESS — e.g., "arranging colorful macarons in a gift box" / "packaging handmade candles" / "assembling a charcuterie board"]. Smooth, fluid movements. Warm natural lighting. ASMR-style — close-up detail shots. The composition is satisfying and symmetrical. 9:16 vertical. 8 seconds. Clean, minimal background.
```

### 3. Text Overlay Hook Video
```
A dramatic 3-second clip of [SCENE — e.g., "a laptop screen showing revenue numbers climbing" / "a coffee cup being set down on a desk at sunrise"]. Cinematic slow motion. Moody lighting with warm golden tones. Slight camera push-in. Designed as a background for text overlay. Dark vignette edges. 9:16 vertical format.
```

### 4. Before & After Transformation
```
A split-screen style 6-second video. Left side: [BEFORE STATE — e.g., "a cluttered desk with papers everywhere"]. Right side: [AFTER STATE — e.g., "the same desk, clean and organized with a laptop and plant"]. The transition between before and after uses a smooth wipe from left to right at the 3-second mark. Bright, clean lighting. 9:16 vertical.
```

### 5. Day in the Life Montage
```
A fast-paced 10-second montage of [PERSONA — e.g., "a small business owner"]. Scene 1: waking up and checking phone (2s). Scene 2: walking into a bright, modern workspace (2s). Scene 3: working at a laptop with coffee (2s). Scene 4: packaging products (2s). Scene 5: smiling at camera with finished products (2s). Natural lighting throughout. Warm, aspirational color grading. 9:16 vertical.
```

### 6. Unboxing Experience
```
Close-up hands opening a [DESCRIBE PACKAGING — e.g., "matte black box with gold foil logo"]. The lid lifts slowly to reveal [YOUR PRODUCT] nestled in tissue paper. Camera angle is slightly above, looking down. Soft studio lighting. The hands pull back the tissue paper to show the product. Luxurious, premium feel. Shallow depth of field. 9:16 vertical. 6 seconds.
```

### 7. POV Tutorial Clip
```
First-person POV shot of someone using [YOUR TOOL/APP/PRODUCT]. The camera shows hands interacting with [SPECIFIC ACTION — e.g., "a phone screen, tapping through an app interface" / "a planner, writing with a gold pen"]. Natural desk setting. Warm afternoon lighting from a window. The movements are smooth and intentional. 9:16 vertical. 5 seconds.
```

## YouTube Shorts

### 8. Hook + Reveal Short
```
A 15-second video that starts with a close-up of [INTRIGUING DETAIL — e.g., "a mysterious glowing object on a dark table"]. At the 3-second mark, the camera quickly zooms out to reveal [THE FULL SCENE — e.g., "an entire room filled with neon-lit products"]. The reveal is dramatic with a slight camera shake. Dynamic lighting that shifts from cool blue to warm amber during the reveal. 9:16 vertical.
```

### 9. Timelapse Creation Video
```
A 10-second timelapse of [CREATION PROCESS — e.g., "building a website on a laptop screen" / "painting a canvas" / "assembling a product"]. Top-down camera angle, perfectly centered. The process speeds up and slows down at key moments for dramatic effect. Clean workspace. Natural daylight with soft shadows. 9:16 vertical format.
```

### 10. Talking Head Background
```
A 5-second looping video of [BACKGROUND SCENE — e.g., "a modern, bright home office with bookshelves, plants, and warm lighting" / "a cozy café interior with steam rising from cups"]. The camera is static but with a very subtle, barely perceptible drift forward. Shallow depth of field to blur the background naturally. Designed as a virtual background for speaking videos. 16:9 horizontal.
```

## Pro Tips for Social Media Videos

1. **First 1-2 seconds matter most** — lead with the most visually striking moment
2. **9:16 vertical** for TikTok, Reels, Shorts, Stories — always specify in your prompt
3. **Keep it short** — 3-10 seconds for clips, 15-60 seconds for full videos
4. **Loop consideration** — request "loopable" for content that benefits from repeat viewing
5. **Text space** — ask for "clean area in center/top for text overlay" when adding captions
6. **Trending aesthetics** change weekly — add current style keywords (cinematic, analog, dreamy)
7. **Aspect ratios matter**: 9:16 (vertical), 16:9 (YouTube), 1:1 (feed posts), 4:5 (Instagram feed)
