## Quick Start (5 Minutes)

### Step 1: Choose Your AI Tool

These prompts work with any major AI assistant:

- **ChatGPT** (chat.openai.com) — Free or Plus
- **Claude** (claude.ai) — Free or Pro
- **Google Gemini** (gemini.google.com) — Free or Advanced
- **Microsoft Copilot** (copilot.microsoft.com) — Free or Pro
- **Any other AI chat tool** — These are standard prompts that work everywhere

### Step 2: Set Your Store Context

Before using any prompt, paste this context block into your AI tool:

```
You are an e-commerce copywriting expert helping me with my online store.

Store: [STORE NAME]
Platform: [SHOPIFY / WOOCOMMERCE / ETSY / AMAZON / BIGCOMMERCE / OTHER]
Niche: [YOUR NICHE — e.g., sustainable fashion, pet accessories, home decor]
Products: [BRIEF DESCRIPTION OF WHAT YOU SELL]
Price range: [LOW-HIGH PRICE RANGE]
Target customer: [WHO BUYS FROM YOU — demographics, interests, pain points]
Brand voice: [TONE — e.g., playful, premium, minimalist, bold, earthy]
Competitors: [1-3 COMPETITOR BRANDS]
Website: [URL]

Keep all copy conversion-focused, on-brand, and optimized for my platform.
```

### Step 3: Copy, Customize, Paste

1. **Copy** the prompt template you want to use.
2. **Replace the placeholders** in `[BRACKETS]` with your actual store information.
3. **Paste** the customized prompt into your AI tool.
4. **Review** the output and refine as needed.

### Step 4: Save Your Customized Versions

Once you've filled in your store details for a prompt, save that version for reuse:
- A Google Doc or Notion page labeled "My Store Prompts"
- A folder organized by category (Product Copy, Emails, Ads, etc.)
- Your project management tool (Trello, Asana, etc.)

## Tips for Best Results

### Be Specific with Placeholders
The more detail you provide in the `[BRACKET]` fields, the better your output. Instead of `[PRODUCT]`, try `[organic bamboo cutting board, 14x10 inches, $34.99, handcrafted in Vermont]`.

### Iterate and Refine
Your first output might be 80% right. Follow up with:
- "Make it more urgent"
- "Shorten to 50 words"
- "Add more sensory language"
- "Write a version for a younger audience"
- "Make it sound more premium"

### Batch Your Work
Set aside dedicated time blocks:
- **Monday:** Generate the week's product descriptions
- **Tuesday:** Write email sequences
- **Wednesday:** Create ad copy variations
- **Thursday:** Build social media content
- **Friday:** Update SEO content and review analytics

### Test Everything
The A/B testing prompts in this kit are some of the most valuable. Use them to create multiple versions, then let your data decide which copy converts best.

### Platform Matters
A product description for Amazon is different from Shopify is different from Etsy. Use the platform-specific prompts to match each channel's buyer expectations.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: Do I need a paid AI subscription?**
No. All prompts work with free tiers of ChatGPT, Claude, and Gemini. Paid plans give longer responses and faster speeds, but are not required.

**Q: Can I use these for multiple stores?**
Yes. Your license covers all stores you personally own and operate.

**Q: What e-commerce platforms do these work for?**
All of them. The prompts are designed to adapt to Shopify, WooCommerce, Amazon, Etsy, BigCommerce, Squarespace, and any other platform. Many prompts include platform-specific variations.

**Q: How often should I refresh my product descriptions?**
Refresh top-selling products quarterly. Update seasonal products monthly. A/B test your highest-traffic listings continuously.

**Q: Can I modify these templates?**
Absolutely. These are yours to customize, combine, and adapt to your brand.

**Q: What if I get a bad output?**
Add more context, be more specific with placeholders, or try a different AI tool. Each AI has slightly different strengths.
