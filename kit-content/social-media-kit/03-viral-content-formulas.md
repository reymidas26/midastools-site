## SECTION 1: TREND JACKING

### 1. Trend Jacking Framework

**Platform:** TikTok, Instagram Reels, X/Twitter
**Best for:** Riding trending topics and formats for maximum reach

```
I create content in the [NICHE] space for [AUDIENCE] on [PLATFORM].

A current trend on [PLATFORM] is: [DESCRIBE THE TREND — format, audio, visual style, or topic]

Create 5 ways I can adapt this trend to my niche:

For each adaptation:
1. Concept description (how I adapt the trend to my business/niche)
2. Hook text or spoken line (first 3 seconds)
3. Full script or caption (step by step what happens in the content)
4. On-screen text overlay
5. Caption + hashtags
6. Why this adaptation works (what makes it feel natural, not forced)
7. Urgency score (1-10: how quickly I need to post this before the trend dies)

My business/brand: [DESCRIBE]
My product/service: [DESCRIBE]
My brand personality: [DESCRIBE]

Also suggest 3 "evergreen trend" formats that I can use any time (not tied to a specific moment):
- Trending audio-agnostic formats that always perform well on [PLATFORM]
```

**Pro Tips:**
- Speed matters — trend jack within 24-48 hours of spotting the trend
- Add your unique twist — don't just copy the trend, add your niche expertise
- Not every trend is for you — skip trends that don't align with your brand

## SECTION 2: CAROUSEL POST FORMULAS

### 4. High-Save Carousel Templates (8 Structures)

**Platform:** Instagram, LinkedIn
**Best for:** Creating carousels that get saved and shared

```
I want to create carousel posts about [TOPIC] for [AUDIENCE] on [PLATFORM].

Create 8 carousel post structures:

STRUCTURE 1: THE LISTICLE CAROUSEL
- Slide 1: Bold statement or number hook ("7 things [AUDIENCE] should stop doing")
- Slides 2-8: One tip per slide (headline + 1-2 sentence explanation)
- Slide 9: Summary slide
- Slide 10: CTA slide ("Follow @[HANDLE] for more" + "Save this post")

STRUCTURE 2: THE BEFORE/AFTER CAROUSEL
- Slide 1: Hook ("I changed [ONE THING] and [RESULT]")
- Slide 2: The "Before" state (relatable pain point)
- Slides 3-6: The changes made (one per slide)
- Slide 7: The "After" state (results)
- Slide 8: CTA + key takeaway

STRUCTURE 3: THE STEP-BY-STEP TUTORIAL
- Slide 1: "How to [ACHIEVE RESULT] in [NUMBER] steps"
- Slides 2-7: One step per slide with clear instructions
- Slide 8: Summary + CTA

STRUCTURE 4: THE MYTH-BUSTING CAROUSEL
- Slide 1: "[NUMBER] myths about [TOPIC] — debunked"
- Slides 2-7: Myth vs. Reality (one per slide)
- Slide 8: "Now you know" + CTA

STRUCTURE 5: THE COMPARISON CAROUSEL
- Slide 1: "[OPTION A] vs [OPTION B] — which is better?"
- Slides 2-7: Side-by-side comparisons on different factors
- Slide 8: Verdict + CTA

STRUCTURE 6: THE STORYTELLING CAROUSEL
- Slide 1: Intriguing hook that starts a story
- Slides 2-8: Story unfolds (one scene per slide)
- Slide 9: Lesson or moral
- Slide 10: CTA

STRUCTURE 7: THE DATA/STATS CAROUSEL
- Slide 1: Shocking or surprising statistic
- Slides 2-6: Supporting data points with visual representations
- Slide 7: What this means for [AUDIENCE]
- Slide 8: CTA

STRUCTURE 8: THE "STARTER PACK" CAROUSEL
- Slide 1: "The [AUDIENCE] starter pack for [GOAL]"
- Slides 2-8: Essential tools, tips, resources, or mindset shifts
- Slide 9: "You're ready" + CTA

For each structure, provide:
- Exact text for every slide
- Design notes (colors, layout, font suggestions)
- Caption to accompany the carousel
- Hashtags

My brand: [DESCRIBE]
My audience: [DESCRIBE]
```

## SECTION 3: THREAD TEMPLATES

### 6. Viral Thread Formulas (X/Twitter)

**Platform:** X/Twitter
**Best for:** Writing threads that get retweeted and bookmarked

```
I want to write a viral thread about [TOPIC] for [AUDIENCE] on X/Twitter.

Create 5 thread templates using these proven formulas:

FORMULA 1: THE "HOW I DID IT" THREAD
- Tweet 1 (Hook): "I [ACHIEVED RESULT] in [TIMEFRAME]. Here's the exact playbook (thread):"
- Tweets 2-9: One step per tweet with specific details
- Tweet 10: Summary + CTA
- Total: 10 tweets

FORMULA 2: THE LESSONS THREAD
- Tweet 1: "[NUMBER] lessons I learned from [EXPERIENCE]"
- Tweets 2-9: One lesson per tweet
- Tweet 10: "Which lesson hit hardest? Reply with the number" + Repost CTA
- Total: 10 tweets

FORMULA 3: THE TOOLS/RESOURCES THREAD
- Tweet 1: "[NUMBER] [TOOLS/RESOURCES] for [ACHIEVING GOAL] — A thread:"
- Tweets 2-9: One tool per tweet (name + what it does + why it's great)
- Tweet 10: "Bookmark this for later. Follow @[HANDLE] for more threads like this."
- Total: 10 tweets

FORMULA 4: THE CONTRARIAN THREAD
- Tweet 1: "Everyone says [COMMON ADVICE]. They're wrong. Here's why:"
- Tweets 2-7: Your counter-argument with evidence
- Tweet 8: What to do instead
- Tweet 9: Summary
- Tweet 10: CTA
- Total: 10 tweets

FORMULA 5: THE BREAKDOWN THREAD
- Tweet 1: "I analyzed [COMPANY/STRATEGY/TREND]. Here's what I found (thread):"
- Tweets 2-9: Key findings and insights
- Tweet 10: "TL;DR:" + 3 bullet points + CTA
- Total: 10 tweets

For each thread:
- Write every tweet in full (max 280 chars each)
- Include where to add images or visuals
- Include the best time to post
- Include 2-3 hashtags for the first tweet only

My expertise: [DESCRIBE]
My Twitter handle: [HANDLE]
```

## SECTION 4: REEL & SHORT-FORM VIDEO SCRIPTS

### 7. Instagram Reel Scripts (10 Formats)

**Platform:** Instagram Reels
**Best for:** Creating Reels that get saves and shares

```
I create Instagram Reels about [NICHE] for [AUDIENCE].

Write 10 complete Reel scripts in these formats:

FORMAT 1: THE TALKING HEAD TIP
- Hook (spoken, 3 sec): "[SURPRISING STATEMENT]"
- Body (20-30 sec): Explain the tip with energy
- CTA (5 sec): "Follow for more [NICHE] tips"
- Text overlay: Key point as on-screen text
- Estimated length: 30-45 seconds

FORMAT 2: THE TRANSITION REVEAL
- Setup (5 sec): Show the "before" or problem
- Transition: Snap, jump cut, or outfit change
- Reveal (10 sec): Show the "after" or solution
- Text overlay: Before → After text
- Estimated length: 15-20 seconds

FORMAT 3: THE "DID YOU KNOW" REEL
- Hook: "Did you know that [SURPRISING FACT]?"
- Explanation: Quick breakdown of why this matters
- CTA: "Save this for later"
- Estimated length: 20-30 seconds

FORMAT 4: THE POV REEL
- On-screen text: "POV: You're a [AUDIENCE] who just [SCENARIO]"
- Visual: Act out the scenario (relatable humor)
- No speaking — let the text and expressions do the work
- Estimated length: 10-15 seconds

FORMAT 5: THE TUTORIAL REEL
- Hook: "Here's how to [RESULT] in 60 seconds"
- Steps: Show each step with text overlays numbered 1-5
- CTA: "Save this and try it yourself"
- Estimated length: 45-60 seconds

FORMAT 6: THE HOT TAKE REEL
- Hook (on-screen text): "Controversial opinion about [NICHE]"
- Speaking: State your hot take with confidence
- Engagement CTA: "Agree or disagree? Comment below"
- Estimated length: 15-30 seconds

FORMAT 7: THE STORY TIME REEL
- Hook: "Story time: [INTRIGUING ONE-LINER]"
- Tell a quick story (30-45 sec) with a twist or lesson
- End with the lesson or takeaway
- Estimated length: 30-60 seconds

FORMAT 8: THE UNBOXING/SHOW & TELL
- Hook: "Let me show you [THING]"
- Walk through 3-5 features or highlights
- End with genuine reaction or recommendation
- Estimated length: 30-45 seconds

FORMAT 9: THE DUET-STYLE REACTION
- React to a trending post or comment in your niche
- Add your expert perspective
- Keep it conversational
- Estimated length: 20-30 seconds

FORMAT 10: THE "STOP DOING THIS" REEL
- Hook: "Stop [COMMON MISTAKE] — here's what to do instead"
- Show the wrong way, then the right way
- CTA: "Share this with someone who needs to hear it"
- Estimated length: 20-30 seconds

For each script, provide:
- Full spoken script (word for word)
- Text overlay directions (what appears on screen and when)
- Suggested trending audio or audio type
- Caption + hashtags

My brand voice: [DESCRIBE]
My products/services: [DESCRIBE]
```

### 8. TikTok Video Scripts (10 Formats)

**Platform:** TikTok
**Best for:** Creating TikToks optimized for the FYP algorithm

```
I create TikTok content about [NICHE] for [AUDIENCE]. My style is [DESCRIBE — e.g., energetic, calm and informative, sarcastic, professional].

Write 10 TikTok video scripts:

1. THE HOOK & TEACH (30 sec)
- First 3 sec: Shocking statement or question
- Next 20 sec: Teach the concept
- Last 7 sec: CTA + loop setup

2. THE GREEN SCREEN EXPLAINER (45 sec)
- Use green screen with article/screenshot
- React and break down what's shown
- Add your expert take

3. THE "STITCH THIS" BAIT (15 sec)
- Say something that invites people to stitch your video
- Ask a question your audience wants to debate
- Short, punchy, opinion-driven

4. THE DAY-IN-THE-LIFE (60 sec)
- Morning routine or workday as a [YOUR ROLE]
- Voiceover with tips woven in
- Aspirational but authentic

5. THE REPLY TO COMMENT (30 sec)
- Start with "Replying to @[USER] who asked [QUESTION]"
- Give a thorough, valuable answer
- CTA: "What else do you want me to cover?"

6. THE TREND ADAPTATION (15-30 sec)
- Take a trending format and make it about your niche
- Match the energy and pacing of the original trend
- Add text overlay with your twist

7. THE RAPID-FIRE TIPS (30 sec)
- "[NUMBER] [TIPS] in 30 seconds — go!"
- One tip every 3-5 seconds
- Fast-paced editing with text overlays

8. THE STORY WITH A TWIST (45 sec)
- Start with an attention-grabbing claim
- Build the story
- Reveal a surprising detail at the end
- Loop back to the hook

9. THE SIDE-BY-SIDE COMPARISON (20 sec)
- Split screen or before/after format
- Compare two approaches, tools, or outcomes
- Let the visual tell the story

10. THE "MOST PEOPLE DON'T KNOW" (30 sec)
- "Most [AUDIENCE] don't know [SECRET/TIP]"
- Reveal the insight
- Explain why it matters
- CTA: "Follow for more [NICHE] secrets"

For each script:
- Full spoken script with timing markers
- Text overlay instructions
- Camera directions (angle, movement, transitions)
- Suggested audio type
- Caption + hashtags
```
