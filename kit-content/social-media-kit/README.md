## How the AI Prompts Work

Each prompt in this kit is formatted in a code block. Copy the entire block, replace the `[BRACKETED PLACEHOLDERS]` with your real information, and paste into your AI tool.

These prompts work with:

- **ChatGPT** (GPT-4 or GPT-4o recommended)
- **Claude** (Claude 3.5 Sonnet or Claude 4 recommended)
- **Google Gemini** (Gemini Advanced recommended)

### Example

```
I manage social media for a [BUSINESS TYPE] targeting [TARGET AUDIENCE]. Our main platforms are [PLATFORMS]. Our brand voice is [DESCRIBE VOICE — e.g., professional but approachable, witty, bold].

Create a 30-day content calendar for [MONTH] with:
- 5 posts per week (Mon-Fri)
- A mix of educational, entertaining, promotional, and engagement posts
- Specific caption hooks for each post
- Recommended hashtags for each post
- Best posting times for my audience
```

Replace the bracketed items with your real details, paste into any AI tool, and get a full month of planned content in minutes.

## Pro Tips for Best Results

1. **Be specific about your brand voice.** The more detail you give the AI about your tone, audience, and goals, the more on-brand the output will be. "Professional but casual, targeting millennial women entrepreneurs" beats "business audience."

2. **Include examples of past top-performing content.** When using these prompts, paste in 2-3 of your best-performing posts so the AI can learn your style and replicate what works.

3. **Iterate and refine.** Your first output is a starting point. Ask the AI to make it punchier, add a stronger hook, include a call-to-action, or adjust the tone.

4. **Batch your content creation.** Use these prompts to create a week or month of content in one sitting. Consistency beats perfection — having 30 days of "good enough" content planned beats posting sporadically.

5. **Adapt prompts across platforms.** A prompt designed for Instagram can be tweaked for LinkedIn or X. The frameworks are transferable — just adjust the tone and format for each platform.
