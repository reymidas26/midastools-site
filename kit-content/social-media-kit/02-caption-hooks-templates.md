## SECTION 1: HOOK FORMULAS

### 1. The "Stop Scrolling" Hook Generator

**Platform:** All platforms
**Best for:** Creating first lines that demand attention

```
I'm writing a social media post about [TOPIC] for [PLATFORM]. My audience is [AUDIENCE].

Generate 10 scroll-stopping hooks using these proven formulas:

1. Controversial opinion: "Unpopular opinion: [STATEMENT]"
2. Number + curiosity: "[NUMBER] [THINGS] that [SURPRISING RESULT]"
3. Story hook: "I [DID SOMETHING] and [UNEXPECTED RESULT happened]"
4. Direct challenge: "Stop [COMMON MISTAKE]. Here's what to do instead:"
5. Social proof: "[RESULT] in [TIMEFRAME]. Here's how:"
6. Fear of missing out: "Everyone is talking about [THING] but nobody mentions [THIS]"
7. Question hook: "Why do [GROUP] always [DO THIS] when [BETTER OPTION] exists?"
8. Myth buster: "You've been told [COMMON ADVICE]. It's wrong."
9. Before/After: "6 months ago I [OLD STATE]. Today I [NEW STATE]. The difference?"
10. Prediction: "In 2 years, [BOLD PREDICTION]. Here's why:"

For each hook, provide:
- The complete hook line
- Why it works psychologically
- Which platform it works best on
- A complete caption built around that hook (150-250 words)

My niche: [NICHE]
My brand voice: [DESCRIBE — e.g., bold and direct, warm and encouraging, witty]
My product/service: [DESCRIBE]
```

## SECTION 2: CAPTION TEMPLATES BY PLATFORM

### 3. Instagram Caption Templates (10 Formats)

**Platform:** Instagram
**Best for:** Writing high-engagement Instagram captions

```
I run an Instagram account for [BUSINESS/NICHE]. My audience is [AUDIENCE]. My brand voice is [DESCRIBE].

Write 10 Instagram captions using these proven formats:

1. THE STORY CAPTION
- Hook with a personal or customer story
- Build tension or curiosity
- Deliver the lesson or insight
- End with a question CTA
- 150-300 words

2. THE LISTICLE CAPTION
- Hook: "[NUMBER] [THINGS] to [ACHIEVE RESULT]"
- List each item with a brief explanation
- End with "Save this for later" CTA
- 100-200 words

3. THE HOT TAKE CAPTION
- Open with a bold, slightly controversial statement
- Back it up with reasoning
- Invite debate: "Agree or disagree? Drop your take below"
- 80-150 words

4. THE EDUCATIONAL CAPTION
- Hook with a problem or question
- Teach one clear concept or strategy
- Use line breaks for readability
- End with "Share this with someone who needs to hear it"
- 150-250 words

5. THE BEHIND-THE-SCENES CAPTION
- Reveal something personal about your business or process
- Be vulnerable or transparent about challenges
- Connect it to a broader lesson
- Ask followers to share their experience
- 100-200 words

6. THE TESTIMONIAL CAPTION
- Quote or paraphrase a customer win
- Add context: what they were struggling with before
- Highlight the transformation
- CTA: "Ready for your own results? Link in bio"
- 100-150 words

7. THE CAROUSEL COMPANION CAPTION
- Hook that complements (not repeats) the carousel content
- Brief summary of what they'll learn by swiping
- CTA: "Swipe through and save for later"
- 80-120 words

8. THE ENGAGEMENT BAIT CAPTION
- Ask a specific, easy-to-answer question
- Give 2-3 options or share your own answer first
- Keep it short and conversational
- 50-80 words

9. THE LAUNCH/PROMO CAPTION
- Hook with the problem your product solves
- Paint the before/after picture
- Introduce your product/service naturally
- Clear CTA with urgency if applicable
- 150-200 words

10. THE REPOST/REINTRODUCTION CAPTION
- "New here? Here's what you need to know about [BRAND]"
- Share your mission, what you do, who you help
- End with "Follow for [CONTENT PROMISE]"
- 100-150 words

For each caption, include:
- The full caption text (ready to copy-paste)
- 10 hashtags (mix of sizes: 3 small <50K, 4 medium 50K-500K, 3 large 500K+)
- Recommended post format (static, carousel, Reel)
- Best day/time to post

Topic for all captions: [YOUR TOPIC OR LEAVE BLANK FOR VARIETY]
```

### 5. LinkedIn Caption Templates

**Platform:** LinkedIn
**Best for:** Thought leadership, professional engagement, B2B content

```
I'm a [YOUR ROLE] who posts on LinkedIn about [TOPICS]. My audience is [DESCRIBE — e.g., startup founders, marketing leaders, HR professionals].

Write 10 LinkedIn posts using these formats:

1. THE PERSONAL STORY POST
- Start with a vulnerable or surprising personal moment
- Connect it to a professional lesson
- End with a question that invites comments
- Format: short paragraphs, line breaks between each thought
- 150-250 words

2. THE FRAMEWORK POST
- Introduce a problem your audience faces
- Present your framework (3-5 steps)
- Use → or numbered list formatting
- End with "Repost if this resonates"
- 200-300 words

3. THE CONTRARIAN TAKE
- Open with "Most [PROFESSIONALS] believe [COMMON BELIEF]. I disagree."
- Explain your alternative viewpoint with evidence
- Invite debate
- 100-200 words

4. THE DATA/INSIGHT POST
- Share a surprising statistic or finding
- Provide your interpretation
- Ask "What are you seeing in your industry?"
- 100-150 words

5. THE "I GOT FIRED/REJECTED/FAILED" POST
- Vulnerable opening about a setback
- What you learned
- How it led to something better
- End with encouragement for others going through it
- 200-300 words

6. THE HIRING/CULTURE POST
- Share an observation about hiring, leadership, or workplace culture
- Take a clear stand
- Use "↳" bullets for key points
- CTA: "Tag a leader who gets this right"
- 100-200 words

7. THE STEP-BY-STEP HOW-TO
- "Here's exactly how I [ACHIEVED RESULT]:"
- Numbered steps with brief explanations
- End with "Follow for more [TOPIC] breakdowns"
- 200-300 words

8. THE CELEBRATION/WIN POST
- Share a milestone or achievement
- Credit the team or journey
- Pull out the lesson for your audience
- 100-150 words

9. THE POLL POST
- Ask a specific professional question
- Provide 4 answer options
- Share your answer in the caption
- 50-80 words + poll options

10. THE CAROUSEL TEXT POST
- "10 things I learned [DOING X]" (text-only list format)
- One insight per line
- Use numbers or → for formatting
- End with "Which one hits hardest? Drop the number below"
- 150-250 words

For each post, include:
- Full post text (ready to paste)
- Best day/time to post
- Predicted engagement trigger (comments, reposts, or saves)
```
