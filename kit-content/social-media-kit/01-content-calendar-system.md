## 1. Complete 30-Day Content Calendar Generator

**Platform:** All platforms
**Best for:** Planning an entire month of content in one session

```
I manage social media for a [BUSINESS TYPE] called [BUSINESS NAME]. Our target audience is [DESCRIBE AUDIENCE — age, interests, pain points]. Our brand voice is [DESCRIBE VOICE — e.g., professional but approachable, witty and bold, warm and educational].

Our main platforms are: [LIST PLATFORMS — e.g., Instagram, TikTok, LinkedIn, X/Twitter]

Create a complete 30-day content calendar for [MONTH/YEAR] with:

For each day, provide:
1. Platform(s) to post on
2. Content type (carousel, reel, static post, story, thread, etc.)
3. Topic/theme
4. Caption hook (first line that stops the scroll)
5. Call-to-action
6. Hashtags (5-10 relevant ones)
7. Best posting time

Weekly theme structure:
- Week 1: [THEME — e.g., Educational / How-to content]
- Week 2: [THEME — e.g., Behind-the-scenes / Personal stories]
- Week 3: [THEME — e.g., Social proof / Testimonials / Results]
- Week 4: [THEME — e.g., Promotional / Sales / Launch content]

Include a mix of:
- 40% educational/value posts
- 20% entertaining/relatable posts
- 20% engagement-driving posts (polls, questions, challenges)
- 20% promotional/CTA posts

Format as a table with columns: Day | Platform | Content Type | Topic | Hook | CTA | Hashtags | Time
```

**Pro Tips:**
- Run this prompt at the start of each month
- Feed in your top 3 performing posts from last month so the AI can build on what works
- Adjust the content mix ratio based on your goals (more promotional during a launch, more educational when growing)

## 2. Instagram-Specific Content Calendar

**Platform:** Instagram
**Best for:** Planning feed posts, Reels, Stories, and carousels

```
I run an Instagram account for [BUSINESS/NICHE] targeting [AUDIENCE]. We currently have [FOLLOWER COUNT] followers and our engagement rate is approximately [X%].

Create a 30-day Instagram content calendar with:

Feed posts (4-5 per week):
- 2 carousel posts (educational or storytelling)
- 1 Reel (entertaining or trending)
- 1 static image post (quote, tip, or testimonial)
- 1 promotional post (product, service, or CTA)

Stories (daily):
- Monday: Poll or quiz
- Tuesday: Behind-the-scenes
- Wednesday: Tip of the day
- Thursday: User-generated content or testimonial
- Friday: Weekend recommendation or personal
- Saturday: Engagement sticker (question box, slider)
- Sunday: Week recap or preview

Reels (3 per week):
- 1 trending audio/format adaptation
- 1 educational (how-to or tutorial)
- 1 entertaining or relatable

For each post provide: Day | Type | Topic | Caption Hook | Hashtags (mix of small/medium/large) | Best Time

Our best-performing content topics have been: [LIST 2-3 TOPICS]
Our brand colors are: [COLORS]
Our products/services: [LIST MAIN OFFERINGS]
```

**Pro Tips:**
- Schedule feed posts using Later, Buffer, or Meta Business Suite
- Film all Reels in one batch session per week
- Use the Stories plan as a template — adapt based on what happens each day

## 3. TikTok Content Calendar

**Platform:** TikTok
**Best for:** Planning short-form video content with trend integration

```
I run a TikTok account in the [NICHE] space targeting [AUDIENCE DEMOGRAPHIC]. Our account currently has [FOLLOWER COUNT] followers.

Create a 30-day TikTok content calendar with 5-7 videos per week. For each video provide:

1. Video concept (1-2 sentence description)
2. Hook (first 3 seconds — what stops the scroll)
3. Format type: talking head / trending sound / green screen / stitch / duet / POV / tutorial / story time
4. Estimated length (15s / 30s / 60s)
5. Caption (short, punchy, with relevant keywords)
6. Hashtags (3-5 niche + 2-3 broad)
7. CTA (follow, comment, share, link in bio)

Content mix:
- 30% trending formats/sounds adapted to our niche
- 25% educational (tips, how-tos, mistakes to avoid)
- 20% entertaining (relatable humor, hot takes, POVs)
- 15% story-driven (customer stories, behind-the-scenes, day-in-the-life)
- 10% promotional (product demos, testimonials, offers)

Our best-performing TikToks have been about: [TOPICS]
Our product/service: [DESCRIBE]
Our brand personality: [DESCRIBE — e.g., sarcastic and fun, professional and informative]
```

**Pro Tips:**
- Film 5-7 TikToks in one batch session
- Always film trending formats within 48 hours of spotting them — speed matters on TikTok
- Keep a running list of trends you see on your FYP and adapt them weekly

## 4. LinkedIn Content Calendar

**Platform:** LinkedIn
**Best for:** B2B content, thought leadership, professional branding

```
I'm a [YOUR ROLE] at [COMPANY/INDUSTRY]. I want to build thought leadership on LinkedIn targeting [AUDIENCE — e.g., startup founders, marketing directors, HR leaders].

Create a 30-day LinkedIn content calendar with 4-5 posts per week:

Post types to include:
- Text-only personal stories (2 per week)
- Carousel posts with frameworks or data (1 per week)
- Poll or engagement post (1 per week)
- Industry insight or hot take (1 per week)

For each post provide:
1. Day of week (best LinkedIn days: Tue, Wed, Thu)
2. Post type (text / carousel / poll / article / video)
3. Topic
4. Opening hook (first 2 lines — must compel "see more" click)
5. Key points to cover
6. Closing CTA (comment, share, follow, DM)
7. Posting time (recommend 7-9am or 12-1pm local time)

Themes by week:
- Week 1: Lessons learned / mistakes made
- Week 2: Industry trends and predictions
- Week 3: Tactical frameworks and how-tos
- Week 4: Personal stories and career reflections

My expertise areas: [LIST 3-5 TOPICS]
My biggest career wins: [LIST 2-3]
Controversial or strong opinions I hold: [LIST 1-2]
```

**Pro Tips:**
- LinkedIn rewards text posts with strong hooks — invest time in your first 2 lines
- Post between 7-9am on Tuesday-Thursday for maximum reach
- Reply to every comment within 1 hour of posting to boost the algorithm

## 5. X/Twitter Content Calendar

**Platform:** X/Twitter
**Best for:** Short-form content, threads, engagement, and real-time commentary

```
I use X/Twitter to build an audience around [TOPIC/NICHE]. My target followers are [AUDIENCE]. I currently have [FOLLOWER COUNT] followers.

Create a 30-day X/Twitter content calendar with 2-3 posts per day:

Daily posting schedule:
- Morning (8-9am): Insight, tip, or hot take
- Midday (12-1pm): Thread or longer-form value post
- Evening (5-7pm): Engagement post (question, poll, or reply-bait)

Weekly content mix:
- Monday: Motivational + Weekly goal setting
- Tuesday: Tutorial thread or how-to
- Wednesday: Hot take or contrarian opinion
- Thursday: Case study or results breakdown
- Friday: Curated resources or recommendations
- Saturday: Personal story or behind-the-scenes
- Sunday: Reflection + preview of next week

For each post provide:
1. Time slot (morning/midday/evening)
2. Format (single tweet / thread / poll / quote tweet prompt)
3. Content (full tweet text, max 280 chars for singles)
4. For threads: provide hook tweet + 5-8 thread tweets + closer with CTA

My content pillars: [LIST 3-4 TOPICS]
My tone: [DESCRIBE — e.g., direct and opinionated, data-driven, irreverent]
```

**Pro Tips:**
- Threads perform best when posted between 8-10am
- Always end threads with a CTA: "Follow for more" or "Repost to share with your audience"
- Engage with 10-20 accounts in your niche before posting to warm up the algorithm

## 6. YouTube Content Calendar

**Platform:** YouTube (long-form + Shorts)
**Best for:** Planning video content, Shorts strategy, and upload schedule

```
I run a YouTube channel about [TOPIC/NICHE] targeting [AUDIENCE]. I currently have [SUBSCRIBER COUNT] subscribers and upload [FREQUENCY — e.g., 1-2 times per week].

Create a 30-day YouTube content calendar with:

Long-form videos (1-2 per week):
- Video title (click-worthy, keyword-optimized)
- Thumbnail concept (text overlay + visual description)
- Hook script (first 30 seconds to retain viewers)
- Key sections/timestamps
- End screen CTA
- Description with SEO keywords
- 3 related video ideas to suggest as "next watch"

YouTube Shorts (3-5 per week):
- Short concept (15-60 seconds)
- Hook (first 3 seconds)
- Script outline
- Trending format or audio to use
- CTA overlay text

Content types to rotate:
- How-to tutorials
- Listicles (Top 5, Top 10)
- Reaction/commentary
- Behind-the-scenes
- Q&A from comments
- Trending topic takes

My best-performing videos have been about: [TOPICS]
My unique angle/expertise: [DESCRIBE]
Keywords I want to rank for: [LIST 5-10]
```

**Pro Tips:**
- Batch film 2-4 videos in one session
- Upload Shorts from your long-form content — repurpose the best 60-second segments
- Titles and thumbnails determine 80% of a video's success — spend time on them

## 7. Weekly Theme Framework Generator

**Platform:** All platforms
**Best for:** Creating a repeatable weekly content structure

```
I create content about [NICHE/TOPIC] for [AUDIENCE] across [PLATFORMS].

Design a weekly content theme framework I can repeat every week. For each day of the week (Monday-Sunday), assign:

1. Theme name (e.g., "Motivation Monday," "Tutorial Tuesday")
2. Content pillar it serves (educational, entertaining, engaging, promotional)
3. Content format (post type best suited for that day)
4. Example topic ideas (3 per day)
5. Platform priority (which platform gets this content first)

Requirements:
- No more than 20% promotional content
- At least 2 engagement-focused days (questions, polls, conversations)
- At least 2 value/educational days
- 1 personal/behind-the-scenes day
- Must feel natural, not forced — avoid overly cute day names if my brand is professional

My brand voice: [DESCRIBE]
My content pillars: [LIST 3-5]
My primary goal: [GROWTH / ENGAGEMENT / SALES / BRAND AWARENESS]
```

## 8. Batch Content Creation Workflow

**Platform:** All platforms
**Best for:** Creating a week or month of content in one session

```
I need to batch-create content for [PLATFORM(S)] for the next [1 WEEK / 2 WEEKS / 1 MONTH].

My content calendar for this period includes:
[PASTE YOUR CONTENT CALENDAR OR LIST OF PLANNED POSTS]

For each planned post, generate:
1. Full caption/copy (ready to post)
2. Visual direction (what the image/video should show)
3. Hashtags (platform-appropriate)
4. Alt text for accessibility
5. Story/Reel companion content (if applicable)

Additional requirements:
- Maintain consistent brand voice: [DESCRIBE VOICE]
- Include CTAs that vary (don't use the same CTA every post)
- Front-load the hook — first line must stop the scroll
- Keep captions under [X WORDS] for [PLATFORM]
- Include 1-2 emoji per post maximum (or none if brand is corporate)

My business: [DESCRIBE]
My products/services: [LIST]
Current promotion or campaign: [DESCRIBE IF ANY]
```

**Pro Tips:**
- Set aside one 3-4 hour block per week for batch creation
- Write all captions first, then create visuals — it's faster
- Use a spreadsheet or Notion board to track what's written vs. scheduled vs. posted

## 10. Seasonal & Holiday Content Planner

**Platform:** All platforms
**Best for:** Planning content around holidays, seasons, and cultural moments

```
My business is a [BUSINESS TYPE] targeting [AUDIENCE]. Our main social media platforms are [PLATFORMS].

Create a seasonal content plan for [QUARTER/MONTH] that includes:

1. All relevant holidays and observances for my audience
2. Industry-specific dates and events
3. Trending seasonal topics and themes
4. For each date/event, provide:
   - 2 content ideas (one educational, one engaging)
   - Caption draft for the primary post
   - Platform recommendation
   - How far in advance to post (day-of vs. lead-up content)

5. "Anti-calendar" ideas — content that stands out by NOT posting about the obvious holiday everyone else is posting about

Our industry: [INDUSTRY]
Our products/services: [LIST]
Upcoming launches or promotions: [LIST IF ANY]
Last year's best-performing seasonal content: [DESCRIBE IF KNOWN]
```

**Pro Tips:**
- Plan holiday content at least 2 weeks in advance
- Not every holiday is relevant to your brand — skip the ones that feel forced
- Create "evergreen" versions of seasonal content you can reuse next year
