## How the AI Prompts Work

Each prompt in this kit is formatted in a code block. Copy the entire block, replace the `[BRACKETED PLACEHOLDERS]` with your real information, and paste into your AI tool.

These prompts work with:

- **ChatGPT** (GPT-4 or GPT-4o recommended)
- **Claude** (Claude 3.5 Sonnet or Claude 4 recommended)
- **Google Gemini** (Gemini Advanced recommended)

### Example

```
I'm applying for a [JOB TITLE] role at [COMPANY NAME]. My background is in [YOUR FIELD] with [X YEARS] of experience. Here is the job description:

[PASTE JOB DESCRIPTION]

Write 5 ATS-optimized bullet points for my experience section that match the keywords in this job description. Use the format: "Action verb + what you did + measurable result."
```

Replace the bracketed items with your real details, paste into any AI tool, and get tailored results in seconds.

## Pro Tips for Best Results

1. **Be specific with placeholders.** The more detail you give the AI, the better the output. "[Managed a team]" gives worse results than "[Managed a team of 12 engineers across 3 time zones]."

2. **Iterate, don't settle.** Your first output is a draft. Ask the AI to refine, make it more concise, add more metrics, or adjust the tone.

3. **Keep a "brag document."** Before using these prompts, spend 30 minutes writing down every accomplishment, metric, project, and win from your career. Feed this to the AI for better outputs.

4. **Customize for every application.** The #1 mistake job seekers make is using the same materials for every job. These prompts are designed to make customization fast and easy.

5. **Use the AI as a collaborator, not a replacement.** The AI generates the structure and language. You add the truth, personality, and specific details that make it yours.
