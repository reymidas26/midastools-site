## How to Use This Kit

### Step 1: Pick Your Prompt
Browse the category files and find the prompt that matches what you need. Each prompt includes context on when and why to use it.

### Step 2: Fill in the Placeholders
Every prompt uses `[PLACEHOLDER]` variables in brackets. Replace these with your specific details:

- `[BRAND NAME]` — Your company or product name
- `[PRODUCT/SERVICE]` — What you sell
- `[TARGET AUDIENCE]` — Who you're writing to
- `[PAIN POINT]` — The problem you solve
- `[BENEFIT]` — The outcome your customer gets
- `[CTA URL]` — Your call-to-action link
- `[PRICE]` — Your pricing
- `[DEADLINE]` — Any time-sensitive dates

### Step 3: Paste Into Any AI Tool
These prompts work with any large language model:

- **ChatGPT** (GPT-4, GPT-4o)
- **Claude** (Anthropic)
- **Gemini** (Google)
- **Llama, Mistral, or any open-source LLM**

Just copy the prompt, fill in your details, and paste it into your preferred AI chat.

### Step 4: Review and Send
AI gives you a professional first draft in seconds. Review the output, tweak the tone to match your brand voice, and drop it into your email platform (Mailchimp, ConvertKit, ActiveCampaign, Klaviyo, etc.).

## Tips for Best Results

### Be Specific with Placeholders
The more detail you put into the placeholders, the better your output. Instead of `[PRODUCT]` = "software", try `[PRODUCT]` = "project management tool for remote marketing teams."

### Stack Multiple Prompts
Use prompts from different files together. Write a welcome sequence (File 01), then create a sales follow-up (File 02), then generate subject line variations (File 06) for A/B testing.

### Iterate and Refine
After the AI generates your first draft, follow up with:
- "Make this more conversational"
- "Add more urgency"
- "Shorten this to 150 words"
- "Write 3 variations of this email"

### Build a Swipe File
Save your best AI-generated emails in a folder. Over time, you'll build a swipe file of proven copy you can reference and remix.

### Customize the Tone
Add tone instructions to any prompt:
- "Write in a friendly, casual tone"
- "Use a professional, authoritative voice"
- "Make this playful and witty"
- "Keep it direct and no-nonsense"

## Email Metrics That Matter

When you send emails written with these prompts, track these KPIs:

- **Open Rate** — Aim for 20-40% (use File 06 for subject line optimization)
- **Click-Through Rate (CTR)** — Aim for 2-5%
- **Reply Rate** — For cold outreach, aim for 5-15%
- **Conversion Rate** — Track purchases, signups, or bookings from each email
- **Unsubscribe Rate** — Keep below 0.5% per send
