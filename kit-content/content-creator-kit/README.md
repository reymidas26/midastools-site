## Quick Start (5 Minutes)

### Step 1: Choose Your AI Tool

These prompts work with any AI assistant. Recommended options:

- **ChatGPT** (any current model) — Best for conversational back-and-forth editing
- **Claude** (Sonnet or Opus) — Best for long-form writing and nuance
- **Gemini** — Good alternative with Google ecosystem integration
- **Any other LLM** — All prompts use standard natural language; no tool-specific syntax

### Step 2: Open Your First Prompt File

Start with the file that matches your most urgent need:

- Need to repurpose a blog post everywhere? Open `01-content-repurposing-engine.md`
- Need a Twitter thread right now? Open `02-twitter-thread-generator.md`
- Filming a video this week? Open `03-youtube-script-builder.md`

### Step 3: Copy, Customize, Paste

1. **Copy** the prompt text from the file
2. **Replace** anything in `[BRACKETS]` with your own details
3. **Paste** into your AI tool
4. **Review** the output and refine as needed

That's it. You're creating content.

## How to Get the Best Results

### Tip 1: Feed the AI Context First

Before using any prompt, give the AI background on your brand. Paste this context block first:

```
CONTEXT ABOUT ME:
- My name is [YOUR NAME]
- I create content about [YOUR NICHE/TOPIC]
- My target audience is [DESCRIBE YOUR AUDIENCE]
- My tone of voice is [casual/professional/witty/authoritative/friendly]
- My content goals are [grow audience/sell product/build authority/educate]
- Platforms I'm active on: [LIST PLATFORMS]
```

This single step will dramatically improve every prompt in this kit.

### Tip 2: Iterate, Don't Settle

The first output is a draft. Always ask follow-up questions:

- "Make the hook more attention-grabbing"
- "Shorten this to under 280 characters"
- "Add more specific examples from [your industry]"
- "Make the tone more casual / more professional"
- "Rewrite the CTA to drive newsletter signups instead"

### Tip 3: Use the Chain Method

The prompts in this kit are designed to work together:

1. Write a blog post or video script (File 03 or 04)
2. Repurpose it across platforms (File 01)
3. Generate threads and social posts (File 02)
4. Plan your publishing calendar (File 05)
5. A/B test headlines (File 06)

### Tip 4: Save Your Best Outputs as Templates

When the AI generates something that matches your voice perfectly, save it. Tell the AI in future sessions: "Write in the style of this example: [paste your saved output]."

## Troubleshooting

| Problem | Solution |
|---------|----------|
| Output sounds generic | Add more context about your niche, audience, and voice |
| Too long / too short | Specify word count in your prompt: "Keep this under 200 words" |
| Wrong tone | Include a sample of your writing and say "Match this tone" |
| AI refuses to write something | Rephrase the request; remove anything that could trigger content filters |
| Output is repetitive | Say "Avoid using the phrases you just used. Give me a completely different angle" |
