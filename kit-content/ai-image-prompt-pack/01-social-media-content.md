# 01 — Social Media Content Prompts

## Instagram Post Images

### 1. Lifestyle Product Feature
**When to use:** Showcasing a product in a real-life setting for an Instagram feed post
**Best for:** ChatGPT (DALL-E), Midjourney

> A beautifully styled flat lay photograph of [PRODUCT] arranged on a [SURFACE MATERIAL — e.g., marble countertop, rustic wooden table, linen fabric]. Surrounded by complementary lifestyle props like [PROP 1 — e.g., fresh eucalyptus sprigs], [PROP 2 — e.g., a ceramic coffee cup], and [PROP 3 — e.g., a pair of reading glasses]. Soft natural window light casting gentle shadows from the left. Color palette: [COLOR PALETTE]. Shot from directly above (bird's eye view), photorealistic, Canon EOS R5, 35mm lens. Aspect ratio 4:5. No text, no watermarks.

**Pro tip:** The 4:5 aspect ratio (1080x1350) takes up maximum vertical space in the Instagram feed, increasing engagement. Always specify "no text" unless you want AI-generated lettering.

**Sizing:** 1080x1350px (4:5) for maximum feed real estate, or 1080x1080px (1:1)

### 2. Carousel Cover Slide
**When to use:** Eye-catching first slide for an educational or tips carousel
**Best for:** ChatGPT (DALL-E), Midjourney

> A bold, modern graphic design for an Instagram carousel cover. [COLOR PALETTE] background with a clean gradient. Large, legible text reading "[CAROUSEL TITLE — e.g., 5 Morning Habits That Changed My Life]" in a bold sans-serif font. Small subtitle text below reading "[SUBTITLE — e.g., Swipe to learn more]". Minimalist decorative elements like subtle geometric shapes or thin accent lines. Professional, influencer-style design. Aspect ratio 4:5. Clean and high contrast.

**Pro tip:** ChatGPT's native image generation renders readable in-image text reliably — use it for carousel covers. Specify your exact font style preference (serif, sans-serif, handwritten) for better results.

**Sizing:** 1080x1350px (4:5)

### 3. Before & After Transformation
**When to use:** Showing results, transformations, or progress
**Best for:** ChatGPT (DALL-E), Stable Diffusion

> A split-image composition showing a dramatic before-and-after transformation. Left side labeled "Before": [BEFORE DESCRIPTION — e.g., a cluttered, dimly lit home office with papers everywhere]. Right side labeled "After": [AFTER DESCRIPTION — e.g., a clean, minimalist home office with organized shelving, a standing desk, and warm ambient lighting]. A clean vertical dividing line separates the two halves. Photorealistic interior photography style, wide-angle lens. Aspect ratio 4:5. Consistent lighting temperature across both halves.

**Pro tip:** Keep the camera angle and perspective identical on both sides for maximum impact. Specify "same room, same angle" for consistency.

**Sizing:** 1080x1350px (4:5)

### 4. Quote Post with Aesthetic Background
**When to use:** Sharing an inspiring quote, testimonial, or stat
**Best for:** ChatGPT (DALL-E)

> An elegant Instagram quote post with a [BACKGROUND STYLE — e.g., soft gradient from dusty rose to cream / blurred nature bokeh / dark moody textured surface] background. Centered white text in a refined [FONT STYLE — e.g., thin serif / bold sans-serif / elegant script] font reading: "[YOUR QUOTE TEXT]". Attribution text below in smaller font reading "— [AUTHOR NAME]". Subtle decorative elements like [DECORATION — e.g., thin gold lines above and below the quote / small botanical illustrations in the corners / quotation mark watermark]. Clean, premium aesthetic. Aspect ratio 1:1.

**Pro tip:** Keep the background simple so the text is readable. If the AI struggles with long quotes, try shortening to under 15 words.

### 5. Photo Dump Grid Aesthetic
**When to use:** Creating a cohesive set of images for a multi-image post
**Best for:** Midjourney, Stable Diffusion

> A [MOOD — e.g., warm, sun-drenched / moody, film-grain / bright and airy] photograph of [SUBJECT — e.g., a cozy coffee shop corner with exposed brick, a half-finished latte, and a worn paperback book on the table]. Shot in a candid, editorial style reminiscent of film photography with [FILM STOCK — e.g., Kodak Portra 400 / Fuji Superia / Cinestill 800T] color grading. **Pro tip:** Generate 5-8 images with the same film stock and mood description to create a cohesive "photo dump" set. Change only the subject for each one.

## Profile Pictures

### 8. Professional Brand Avatar
**When to use:** Creating a consistent, recognizable profile picture
**Best for:** ChatGPT (DALL-E), Midjourney

> A professional profile picture of a [GENDER] [AGE RANGE] [ETHNICITY] person with [HAIR DESCRIPTION] and [CLOTHING — e.g., a navy blazer over a white crew neck]. Warm, confident smile. Shot against a [BACKGROUND — e.g., soft blurred neutral gradient / clean white / warm-toned bokeh]. Studio lighting with a key light at 45 degrees and subtle fill light. Shot on an 85mm portrait lens, f/2.0, shallow depth of field. The subject fills the circular crop area well. Photorealistic, editorial portrait quality. Aspect ratio 1:1.

**Pro tip:** Specify the exact crop framing: "head and shoulders, centered in frame, slight left turn of the head" to control how it looks when cropped to a circle.

**Sizing:** 400x400px minimum (1:1), platforms auto-crop to circle

## YouTube Thumbnails

### 12. Reaction/Emotion YouTube Thumbnail
**When to use:** Creating thumbnails that drive clicks through emotional expression
**Best for:** ChatGPT (DALL-E), Midjourney

> A YouTube thumbnail showing a [GENDER] person with an exaggerated [EMOTION — e.g., shocked / excited / skeptical] facial expression. They are [POSE — e.g., holding their hands on their cheeks / pointing at something off-screen / covering their mouth in surprise]. Background: [BACKGROUND — e.g., bright solid red with radiating yellow speed lines / blurred luxury interior]. Bold, thick text reading "[TITLE — e.g., I CAN'T BELIEVE THIS WORKED]" in white with black outline, positioned in the right half. The person is positioned on the left third. Dramatic, punchy lighting. Photorealistic. Aspect ratio 16:9.

**Pro tip:** YouTube thumbnails are viewed at roughly 320x180px. Use a face taking up at least 30% of the frame, max 5 words of text, and only 2-3 colors. Test your thumbnail at thumbnail size before publishing.

**Sizing:** 1280x720px (16:9)

**When to use:** "Top 10" or "Best of" style video thumbnails

Professional, tech-reviewer aesthetic. High production value feel. Aspect ratio 16:9. Sharp, vivid colors.

## Social Media Ad Creatives

**When to use:** Paid social ad creative for feed placement
**Best for:** ChatGPT (DALL-E), Midjourney

> A high-converting social media ad image for [PRODUCT/SERVICE]. Clean, premium composition with [PRODUCT/VISUAL] as the hero element, positioned in the left two-thirds of the frame. **When to use:** First slide of a swipeable carousel ad

### 25. User-Generated Content Style Ad
**When to use:** Creating ads that look organic and native to the feed
**Best for:** Midjourney, ChatGPT (DALL-E)

A [PERSON DESCRIPTION — e.g., woman in her late 20s with natural makeup] casually [ACTION — e.g., holding up / unboxing / using] [PRODUCT] in a [SETTING — e.g., cozy apartment living room / bright kitchen / outdoor cafe].
