# 05 — Professional Headshots & Portraits

25+ prompts for headshots, team photos, author photos, and personal brand photography.

## Professional Headshots — Corporate

### 1. Classic Corporate Headshot
**When to use:** LinkedIn profile, company website team page, corporate directory
**Best for:** ChatGPT (DALL-E), Midjourney

> A professional corporate headshot of a [GENDER] [AGE RANGE — e.g., mid-30s] [ETHNICITY] professional with [HAIR DESCRIPTION — e.g., short dark brown hair, neatly styled] wearing a [OUTFIT — e.g., charcoal gray suit with a light blue dress shirt, no tie / navy blazer over a white blouse]. Warm, confident expression with a genuine, approachable smile. Head and shoulders framing, subject slightly off-center (rule of thirds). Background: [BACKGROUND — e.g., soft gradient from medium gray to light gray / slightly blurred modern office / clean neutral wall with subtle warm tone]. Studio lighting: large softbox at 45 degrees camera-right (key light), fill light camera-left at half intensity, subtle hair light from behind. Shot on an 85mm portrait lens, f/2.8, shallow depth of field. Color-accurate, professional headshot photography. Aspect ratio 3:4.

**Pro tip:** The 85mm focal length is the industry standard for headshots — it flatters facial proportions and creates beautiful background separation. Always specify it.

**Sizing:** 800x1000px (3:4) for LinkedIn; 1:1 square crop for most other platforms

### 2. Executive / C-Suite Headshot
**When to use:** CEO, founder, or executive team page with a commanding presence
**Best for:** Midjourney, ChatGPT (DALL-E)

> A powerful executive headshot of a [GENDER] [AGE RANGE] [ETHNICITY] CEO or founder. Wearing [OUTFIT — e.g., a perfectly tailored dark navy suit with a crisp white shirt and understated silk tie / a structured black blazer with a minimal gold necklace]. Expression: confident, poised, and commanding — a leader's presence. Not smiling broadly — a subtle, knowing expression that conveys authority. Arms crossed or one hand on the chin in a thoughtful pose. Background: [BACKGROUND — e.g., dark charcoal gradient creating a dramatic, editorial mood / a blurred floor-to-ceiling window with city skyline views / a textured dark wall]. Dramatic Rembrandt-style lighting with strong key light creating a defined shadow pattern on one side of the face. Shot on a 100mm lens, f/2.0. Rich, contrasty color grade. Magazine-cover quality. Aspect ratio 3:4.

**Pro tip:** Executive headshots should feel like magazine covers. Dramatic lighting (Rembrandt or split lighting), dark backgrounds, and a powerful expression set the tone. Avoid casual smiles.

### 3. Approachable Corporate Headshot
**When to use:** Customer-facing roles, sales teams, about pages where warmth matters
**Best for:** ChatGPT (DALL-E), Midjourney

> A warm, approachable professional headshot of a [GENDER] [AGE RANGE] [ETHNICITY] professional with [HAIR DESCRIPTION] wearing [OUTFIT — e.g., a soft blue button-down / a warm cream sweater / a casual blazer over a t-shirt]. Genuine, warm smile with visible teeth — the kind of smile that makes you want to work with this person. Head slightly tilted, body turned 30 degrees from camera with face toward the lens. Background: [BACKGROUND — e.g., bright, airy, slightly blurred indoor setting with natural light / a warm neutral gradient / soft green foliage bokeh]. Natural window light from one side supplemented with a subtle reflector fill. Bright, warm color temperature. Shot on a 70mm lens, f/3.5. Professional but not stiff. Aspect ratio 3:4.

**Pro tip:** For customer-facing roles, warmth beats authority. A genuine smile, natural lighting, and slightly casual attire make the person look trustworthy and approachable.

### 4. Women in Leadership Headshot
**When to use:** Female executive, founder, or professional headshot
**Best for:** ChatGPT (DALL-E), Midjourney

> A striking professional headshot of a [AGE RANGE] [ETHNICITY] woman in a leadership role. [HAIR DESCRIPTION — e.g., shoulder-length dark hair worn down / short natural curls / pulled-back professional updo]. Wearing [OUTFIT — e.g., a structured emerald green blazer / a tailored all-black ensemble / a silk blouse in jewel tones]. Expression: confident, direct eye contact, a slight smile that communicates competence and warmth simultaneously. Head and shoulders framing. Background: [BACKGROUND — e.g., rich dark gradient / softly lit modern office / clean neutral tone]. Lighting: butterfly lighting (light directly in front and above) creating flattering, even illumination with a subtle shadow under the nose. Shot on an 85mm lens. Color palette emphasizes rich, saturated tones. Aspect ratio 3:4.

**Pro tip:** Butterfly lighting (also called Paramount lighting) is universally flattering and has been the go-to for Hollywood portraits for decades. It minimizes undereye shadows and defines cheekbones.

## Professional Headshots — Creative

### 5. Creative Industry Headshot
**When to use:** Designers, marketers, writers, or other creative professionals
**Best for:** Midjourney, ChatGPT (DALL-E)

> A creative professional headshot of a [GENDER] [AGE RANGE] [ETHNICITY] [PROFESSION — e.g., designer / creative director / photographer]. Wearing [OUTFIT — e.g., a black turtleneck / a denim jacket over a graphic tee / an oversized linen shirt]. Expression: relaxed confidence, a natural half-smile, slightly intense eye contact. The pose is more relaxed than corporate — [POSE — e.g., leaning against a wall with arms casually folded / sitting on a stool with one hand on the knee / standing with hands in pockets]. Background: [BACKGROUND — e.g., exposed brick wall with warm tones / a creative studio with blurred work in progress / an urban exterior with clean architecture]. Natural light supplemented with a warm continuous light source. Slightly editorial, magazine-feature quality. Film-inspired color grade with subtle grain. Shot on a 50mm lens, f/1.8. Aspect ratio 3:4.

**Pro tip:** Creative headshots break rules on purpose. A wider lens (50mm vs 85mm), environmental backgrounds, and casual poses signal "I'm creative, not corporate." Match the vibe to your industry.

## Team Photos

### 8. Small Team Group Photo
**When to use:** Company about page or team section (3-8 people)
**Best for:** ChatGPT (DALL-E), Midjourney

> A natural, candid group photograph of a [NUMBER]-person diverse team in a [SETTING — e.g., modern open-plan office / outdoor courtyard / bright meeting room with glass walls]. The team stands in a relaxed, asymmetric grouping — not in a rigid line. Team members wear [DRESS CODE — e.g., smart casual / business casual / creative casual] clothing. Everyone has genuine, natural expressions — some mid-laugh, some warm smiles. The composition feels candid, like a moment captured during a break. Natural light from large windows supplemented with soft fill flash. Warm, authentic color grade. Environmental portrait style on a 35mm lens, f/4 for full group sharpness. Aspect ratio 16:9.

**Pro tip:** The best team photos look candid, not posed. Ask someone to tell a joke right before the shot. Staggered heights (some sitting, some standing, some leaning) create visual interest.

### 9. Team Working / Collaboration Shot
**When to use:** Showing your team in action for culture content or careers pages
**Best for:** ChatGPT (DALL-E), Midjourney

> A candid photograph of a diverse team of [NUMBER] people collaborating around a [SETTING — e.g., whiteboard covered in colorful sticky notes / large conference table with laptops and notebooks / standing desk area with dual monitors]. The people are genuinely engaged — [ACTIONS — e.g., one person is sketching on the whiteboard while two others discuss, a fourth takes notes / they're pointing at a screen and debating ideas / passing around product prototypes]. Natural body language, authentic interaction. The space is [OFFICE STYLE — e.g., bright modern startup / creative loft with exposed beams / warm coworking space]. Warm, natural lighting from windows. Documentary-style photography, slightly desaturated for an authentic editorial feel. 35mm lens, candid perspective. Aspect ratio 16:9.

**Pro tip:** Collaboration photos should show REAL work artifacts — whiteboards with actual ideas (even if approximated), screens with interfaces, sketches in hand. Empty-table team photos look like stock photography.

### 10. Remote Team Virtual Photo
**When to use:** Distributed teams that can't be in the same location
**Best for:** ChatGPT (DALL-E)

> A creative virtual team photo concept showing [NUMBER] individual headshot frames arranged in a [LAYOUT — e.g., Zoom-style grid / creative mosaic / floating circular bubbles] against a [BACKGROUND — e.g., brand-colored gradient / dark sophisticated background / abstract network visualization connecting each person]. Each frame shows a different team member: diverse in [DESCRIPTION — e.g., age, ethnicity, gender, backgrounds]. Each person is in their own unique environment (home office, cafe, co-working space) but all have warm, friendly expressions. **Pro tip:** For real remote teams, have everyone take their headshot with the same lighting direction and crop, then compose them together. Consistency in individual photos makes the composite look intentional.

## Author Photos

### 11. Book Author Photo — Serious/Literary
**When to use:** Back cover author photo for non-fiction or literary works
**Best for:** Midjourney, ChatGPT (DALL-E)

> A literary author portrait of a [GENDER] [AGE RANGE] [ETHNICITY] writer. [APPEARANCE — e.g., wearing a dark wool sweater, reading glasses pushed up on their head / in a tweed blazer with an open-collar shirt / casual but intentional styling]. Expression: thoughtful, intelligent, slightly intense — the look of someone with important ideas. [POSE — e.g., chin resting lightly on one hand / arms folded, leaning against a bookshelf / seated at a desk with a manuscript visible]. Background: [BACKGROUND — e.g., a wall of bookshelves, softly blurred / a dimly lit study with warm lamp light / a window with rain]. Warm, moody lighting from one side — window light or practical lamp light. Rich, slightly desaturated color palette. Shot on a 105mm lens for a compressed, intimate feel. Aspect ratio 3:4.

**Pro tip:** Author photos should match the book's tone. Thriller writers: darker, more intense. Self-help: warm and approachable. Literary fiction: contemplative and moody. The photo IS a book recommendation.

## Profile Pictures for Different Platforms

### 13. LinkedIn Profile Picture
**When to use:** Optimized specifically for LinkedIn's circular crop and professional context
**Best for:** ChatGPT (DALL-E), Midjourney

> A LinkedIn-optimized professional headshot of a [GENDER] [AGE RANGE] [ETHNICITY] professional. [OUTFIT — e.g., navy blazer with white shirt / professional blouse in a rich color]. Head and shoulders tightly cropped, face centered for circular crop compatibility. The face fills approximately 60% of the frame. Background: a simple, non-distracting [BACKGROUND — e.g., soft blue-gray gradient / blurred bright office / clean neutral tone]. Warm, natural-looking lighting — key light at 30 degrees, gentle fill. The expression is professional yet approachable — a warm half-smile with direct eye contact. Color palette skews toward blue and neutral tones (LinkedIn's brand feel). 85mm lens, f/2.8. Aspect ratio 1:1.

**Pro tip:** LinkedIn crops to a circle and displays at very small sizes. Fill the frame with your face (60%+), use a high-contrast background, and ensure your face is centered. Test the crop at 50x50px.

**Sizing:** 400x400px minimum, 1:1

**Best for:** ChatGPT (DALL-E), Midjourney

[OUTFIT — e.g., trendy casual — a fitted solid-color tee / stylish layered outfit / bold patterned shirt]. Vibrant, energetic expression — a natural laugh or big genuine smile. ### 15. YouTube Channel Profile / Avatar
**When to use:** YouTube channel profile picture
**Best for:** ChatGPT (DALL-E), Midjourney
