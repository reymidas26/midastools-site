## Ghibli / Anime Style

### 1. Studio Ghibli Landscape
**When to use:** Creating dreamy, painterly landscape images with that unmistakable Ghibli warmth
**Best for:** Midjourney, ChatGPT (DALL-E)

> A breathtaking landscape in the style of Studio Ghibli. [SCENE — e.g., a rolling green hillside with wildflowers overlooking a distant seaside village / a dense enchanted forest with massive ancient trees and dappled sunlight / a countryside train winding through rice paddies with mountains in the background]. Towering cumulus clouds in a vivid blue sky with warm golden sunlight. The grass and vegetation sways gently in the breeze. Hand-painted animation quality with visible brushwork in the sky and foliage. Warm, saturated color palette dominated by greens, blues, and golden yellows. Sense of wonder and tranquility. No characters, pure environment. Hayao Miyazaki's visual storytelling. Aspect ratio 16:9.

**Pro tip:** The Ghibli magic is in the clouds, light, and vegetation. Always specify "towering cumulus clouds" and "warm golden sunlight" — these are the signature Ghibli elements.

### 6. Film Noir Style
**When to use:** Dramatic, moody, black-and-white or high-contrast scenes
**Best for:** Midjourney, ChatGPT (DALL-E)

> A classic film noir scene. [SCENE — e.g., a lone figure in a trench coat and fedora standing under a single streetlamp on a wet cobblestone street / a mysterious person seated at a dimly lit bar, cigarette smoke curling through a shaft of light / a shadow cast dramatically on a venetian blind-striped wall]. ## Hyperrealistic Photography

**When to use:** Food content that looks so real it's practically edible
**Best for:** Midjourney, ChatGPT (DALL-E)

> Hyperrealistic food photography of [DISH — e.g., a freshly baked sourdough loaf with a crackling golden crust, just sliced to reveal the airy crumb / a decadent chocolate lava cake with molten center flowing onto a ceramic plate / a perfectly layered poke bowl with glistening fresh sashimi].
