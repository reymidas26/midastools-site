# 02 — Brand & Marketing Prompts

25+ prompts for building a cohesive visual brand — from logos to presentations.

---

## Logo Concepts & Brand Identity

### 1. Minimalist Wordmark Logo
**When to use:** Creating a clean, text-based logo concept
**Best for:** ChatGPT (DALL-E), Midjourney

> A minimalist wordmark logo for "[BRAND NAME]", a [INDUSTRY — e.g., modern wellness brand / fintech startup / creative agency]. The typography is [FONT STYLE — e.g., geometric sans-serif / elegant thin serif / rounded friendly sans-serif] with a subtle design twist on the letter "[LETTER — e.g., the 'A' is replaced with a triangular geometric shape]". Color: [PRIMARY COLOR] on a clean white background. The logo conveys [BRAND ATTRIBUTES — e.g., trust, innovation, simplicity]. Flat vector style, professional logo design quality. No gradients, no shadows. Aspect ratio 3:1.

**Pro tip:** Generate multiple variations and use them as starting inspiration for a vector recreation in Illustrator or Canva. AI logos are great for concept exploration, not final files.

---

### 2. Icon/Symbol Logo Mark
**When to use:** Creating a standalone icon or symbol for your brand
**Best for:** Midjourney, ChatGPT (DALL-E)

> A professional logo icon mark for a [INDUSTRY] company called "[BRAND NAME]". The icon cleverly combines [ELEMENT 1 — e.g., a mountain] and [ELEMENT 2 — e.g., an upward arrow] into a single, unified geometric shape. Clean vector style with [COLOR PALETTE — e.g., a gradient from ocean blue to teal / flat single color in deep navy]. Simple enough to work at 16x16 favicon size. The design suggests [BRAND VALUES — e.g., growth, nature, aspiration]. White background, centered, no text. Aspect ratio 1:1.

**Pro tip:** A great logo icon should be recognizable as a silhouette. After generating, convert it to pure black and check if it still reads clearly. If not, simplify.

---

### 3. Brand Color Palette Visualization
**When to use:** Exploring color palettes for a brand identity
**Best for:** ChatGPT (DALL-E), Midjourney

> A professional brand color palette presentation. Five color swatches arranged horizontally as tall rounded rectangles on a clean white background. Colors: [COLOR 1 — primary], [COLOR 2 — secondary], [COLOR 3 — accent], [COLOR 4 — neutral light], [COLOR 5 — neutral dark]. Each swatch has its hex code printed below in a clean sans-serif font. Below the swatches, a small mood board strip showing the colors applied to abstract shapes, typography samples, and UI elements. Elegant, design-agency presentation style. Aspect ratio 16:9.

**Pro tip:** Use this to visualize palettes before committing. Test your colors for accessibility contrast ratios (WCAG AA) — pretty doesn't always mean readable.

---

### 4. Brand Guidelines Mockup Page
**When to use:** Creating a professional brand guidelines page for client presentation or internal use
**Best for:** ChatGPT (DALL-E)

> A single page from a professional brand guidelines document for "[BRAND NAME]". Clean white page with a grid layout showing: the logo in [COLOR] (top left), the logo in white on dark background (top right), color palette swatches with hex codes (middle section), primary and secondary typography specimens showing [FONT NAME STYLE — e.g., a geometric sans-serif] in various weights (bottom section). Subtle grid lines and generous whitespace. Minimalist, high-end design agency quality. Aspect ratio 3:4 (portrait page).

**Pro tip:** These are perfect for impressing clients or stakeholders. Pair with your actual brand values and voice guidelines in a PDF for a complete brand kit.

## Product Mockups & Packaging

### 5. Product Box Packaging Mockup
**When to use:** Visualizing physical product packaging before production
**Best for:** Midjourney, ChatGPT (DALL-E)

> A photorealistic 3D product packaging mockup of a [PRODUCT TYPE — e.g., premium skincare box / tech gadget box / supplement container]. The packaging features [DESIGN — e.g., matte black with gold foil lettering / clean white with botanical illustrations / bold gradient colors]. Brand name "[BRAND NAME]" prominently displayed in [FONT STYLE]. The box is positioned at a three-quarter angle on a [SURFACE — e.g., white marble countertop / clean seamless background / natural wooden shelf]. Soft studio lighting with subtle reflections. Photorealistic product photography quality. Aspect ratio 4:5.

**Pro tip:** Specify the packaging material ("matte finish", "glossy UV coating", "kraft paper", "embossed texture") for dramatically more realistic results.

### 6. T-Shirt / Apparel Mockup
**When to use:** Previewing a design on clothing
**Best for:** Midjourney, ChatGPT (DALL-E)

> A realistic t-shirt mockup photograph. A [GENDER] model with a [BUILD — e.g., athletic / average] build wearing a [COLOR — e.g., vintage black / heather gray / white] crew neck t-shirt with a [DESIGN DESCRIPTION — e.g., minimalist line drawing of a mountain range / bold typographic print reading "[TEXT]" / small embroidered logo on the left chest]. Natural outdoor lighting, [SETTING — e.g., city street / studio / nature]. Lifestyle brand lookbook photography style. Aspect ratio 4:5.

### 7. Digital Product Mockup (Phone/Laptop)
**When to use:** Showing an app, website, or digital product on a device
**Best for:** ChatGPT (DALL-E), Midjourney

> A premium device mockup showing [DIGITAL PRODUCT — e.g., a mobile app interface / a website landing page / a SaaS dashboard] on a [DEVICE — e.g., latest MacBook Pro / iPhone 15 Pro / iPad Pro]. The screen is bright and clearly legible. Photorealistic, tech product photography. Aspect ratio 16:9.

### 8. Packaging Unboxing Scene
**When to use:** Creating unboxing-style content for social or e-commerce
**Best for:** Midjourney, ChatGPT (DALL-E)

> A beautiful overhead flat lay of an unboxing experience. [PRODUCT] partially removed from a [BOX STYLE — e.g., minimalist white box with embossed logo / rustic kraft paper box with twine / premium matte black magnetic closure box]. Inside: [CONTENTS — e.g., tissue paper in brand color, a thank-you card, the product wrapped in cloth]. Scattered around the box: [PROPS — e.g., dried flowers, brand stickers, a small sample sachet]. Aspect ratio 1:1.

**Pro tip:** Unboxing content is one of the most shared types of product content. Show layers — tissue paper, cards, sachets — to communicate premium quality.

## Website Hero Images & Banners

### 9. SaaS Landing Page Hero
**When to use:** Hero image for a software or tech website
**Best for:** ChatGPT (DALL-E), Midjourney

> A modern SaaS website hero image. Abstract, flowing 3D shapes in [COLOR PALETTE] floating against a [BACKGROUND — e.g., soft gradient from deep purple to midnight blue / clean white with subtle grid]. The shapes suggest [CONCEPT — e.g., connectivity, data flow, automation, growth] without being literal. Subtle particle effects and soft glowing light trails weave between the shapes. The composition leaves generous negative space on the [SIDE — left/right] for headline text placement. Premium, Silicon Valley tech aesthetic. Depth of field effect. Aspect ratio 16:9.

**Pro tip:** Always leave one side empty for your headline text. Specify "negative space on the left for text" or "right side clean for copy placement."

---

### 10. E-Commerce Hero Banner
**When to use:** Main banner on an online store homepage
**Best for:** ChatGPT (DALL-E), Midjourney

> A lifestyle hero banner for an e-commerce [PRODUCT CATEGORY — e.g., home decor / fashion / outdoor gear] brand. A beautifully staged scene showing [SCENE — e.g., a sunlit living room with a model lounging on a couch surrounded by the brand's throw pillows and candles / a model walking through a golden wheat field wearing the brand's clothing line]. Warm, aspirational lighting with a slightly desaturated, editorial color grade. The left third of the image is intentionally darker or simpler for headline text overlay. Cinematic, high-end editorial photography. Shot on medium format, shallow depth of field. Aspect ratio 21:9 (ultra-wide).

**Pro tip:** Ultra-wide (21:9) hero banners look stunning on desktop. Generate a separate 1:1 or 9:16 version for mobile. Never use the same crop for both.

### 11. Service Business Hero Image
**When to use:** Hero for a consulting, agency, or professional services website

> A professional hero image for a [SERVICE TYPE — e.g., marketing agency / law firm / accounting practice / design studio] website. [SCENE — e.g., a modern, bright conference room with a diverse team collaborating around a glass table with laptops and whiteboards / a confident professional standing in a contemporary office with city views]. Slightly desaturated corporate color grade. Strong negative space in the [SIDE] half for text overlay. Aspect ratio 16:9.

> A [SEASON/OCCASION — e.g., Summer Sale / Black Friday / Valentine's Day / Spring Collection] promotional banner. [BACKGROUND — e.g., warm coral-to-peach gradient with scattered tropical leaf shadows / dark dramatic background with gold metallic confetti particles / soft pink with floating heart-shaped bokeh]. **Pro tip:** Generate both landscape (16:9 for desktop banners) and square (1:1 for mobile/email) versions with the same visual language.

## Email Header Graphics

### 13. Welcome Email Header
**When to use:** Header graphic for onboarding or welcome email sequences
**Best for:** ChatGPT (DALL-E)

> A warm, inviting email header graphic for a welcome email from [BRAND NAME]. [BACKGROUND — e.g., soft gradient in brand colors / watercolor wash / clean flat color]. Centered text reading "[TEXT — e.g., Welcome to the family! / You're in! / Let's get started]" in a friendly, warm [FONT STYLE] font. [VISUAL ELEMENTS — e.g., confetti particles / a small illustrated gift box / waving hand emoji-style illustration / botanical border]. The design feels personal, not corporate. Warm, optimistic energy. Aspect ratio 3:1 (600x200px email width).

**Pro tip:** Email headers should be under 100KB for fast loading. Keep the design simple — complex images render inconsistently across email clients.

**Sizing:** 600x200px (3:1)

---

### 14. Newsletter Header
**When to use:** Recurring header for weekly/monthly newsletters
**Best for:** ChatGPT (DALL-E)

> A branded newsletter header for "[NEWSLETTER NAME]" by [BRAND NAME]. Clean, consistent design with the newsletter title in [FONT STYLE — e.g., bold serif / modern sans-serif] on the left, and a subtle [VISUAL — e.g., abstract pattern / small brand icon / issue number badge] on the right. Color palette: [COLOR PALETTE]. A thin accent line or border below. The design is professional yet has personality — not a corporate memo. It should be instantly recognizable in an inbox. Aspect ratio 3:1. Simple, fast-loading design.

**Pro tip:** Consistency is key. Use the exact same header every issue — it builds brand recognition. Change only the issue number or date.

**Sizing:** 600x200px (3:1)

### 15. Promotional Email Banner
**When to use:** Sale announcement, product launch, or special offer email
**Best for:** ChatGPT (DALL-E)

> A high-impact promotional email banner for [PROMOTION — e.g., a flash sale / new product launch / seasonal offer]. [BACKGROUND — e.g., bold brand color gradient / product lifestyle photography with overlay / dynamic geometric pattern]. Large headline: "[HEADLINE — e.g., NEW ARRIVAL / 48-HOUR FLASH SALE]" in bold white text with subtle shadow. [PRODUCT IMAGE or visual] centered or to one side. A clear CTA button shape reading "[CTA — e.g., Shop Now / Get 40% Off / Claim Your Discount]" at the bottom. Polished, brand-consistent, conversion-focused. Aspect ratio 5:2.

## Presentation Slide Backgrounds

### 16. Title Slide Background
**When to use:** Opening slide of a keynote, pitch deck, or presentation
**Best for:** ChatGPT (DALL-E), Midjourney

> A dramatic, cinematic presentation title slide background. [BACKGROUND — e.g., deep navy to black gradient with subtle scattered light particles / aerial photograph of a vast mountain landscape with mist / abstract 3D topographic terrain in dark teal]. The overall mood is [MOOD — e.g., inspiring, bold, premium, forward-looking]. The composition has a clear focal point in the [POSITION — e.g., center / upper third] with the rest fading into subtle darkness, leaving ample space for title text. No text on the image. Cinematic aspect ratio 16:9. High resolution, suitable for large projection.

**Pro tip:** Dark backgrounds with light text project better in conference rooms and on stage. Always test your slides on a projector — screens lie.

**Sizing:** 1920x1080px (16:9)

---

### 17. Section Divider Slide Background
**When to use:** Transitional slides between presentation sections
**Best for:** ChatGPT (DALL-E), Midjourney

> A clean, modern presentation section divider background. [BACKGROUND — e.g., a bold block of brand color covering the left 40% with clean white on the right / a soft abstract gradient with subtle geometric lines / a minimal photographic texture like marble, concrete, or fabric]. The design creates a clear visual break without being distracting. Generous negative space for a short section title. Cohesive with a [STYLE — e.g., corporate / creative / tech startup] presentation theme. Subtle, not busy. No text. Aspect ratio 16:9.

**Pro tip:** Use the same section divider template throughout your deck but change the accent color for each section. This creates visual rhythm without chaos.

**Sizing:** 1920x1080px (16:9)

---

### 18. Content Slide Background
**When to use:** Background for data, charts, or text-heavy content slides
**Best for:** ChatGPT (DALL-E)

> A minimal, clean presentation background designed to support text and data content. [BACKGROUND — e.g., pure white with a subtle light gray geometric pattern in the bottom-right corner / very soft off-white with thin horizontal pinstripe lines / light cream with a barely visible watercolor wash]. A subtle accent element in [BRAND COLOR] along the [POSITION — e.g., bottom edge / left margin / top-right corner] for brand continuity. The design is intentionally understated — the content is the star, not the background. Professional, polished, distraction-free. No text. Aspect ratio 16:9.

**Pro tip:** Content slides should have backgrounds so subtle they're almost invisible. If the background competes with a chart or bullet points, it's too much. Less is more.

**Sizing:** 1920x1080px (16:9)

---

### 19. Pitch Deck — Problem Slide Background
**When to use:** The "problem" or "pain point" slide in a startup pitch deck
**Best for:** ChatGPT (DALL-E), Midjourney

> A moody, tension-filled presentation background for a "problem" slide. [BACKGROUND — e.g., dark, stormy clouds with dramatic lighting breaking through / a chaotic, tangled network of red threads on a dark surface / a cluttered, overwhelming desk shot from above with harsh overhead lighting]. The mood conveys [EMOTION — e.g., frustration, complexity, being overwhelmed, wasted time]. Dark overall tone with one point of light or hope. The feeling should make the audience uncomfortable — setting up the solution. No text. Aspect ratio 16:9.

### 23. Social Media Brand Template Set
**When to use:** Creating a cohesive set of templates across platforms
**Best for:** ChatGPT (DALL-E)

> A set of four cohesive social media templates for [BRAND NAME], displayed as a flat lay arrangement. All four share the same [COLOR PALETTE] and [FONT STYLE]. Template 1 (top-left): Instagram post (1:1) with product feature layout. Template 2 (top-right): Instagram Story (9:16) with quote layout. ### 25. Branded Invoice / Receipt Design
**When to use:** Creating a beautiful, on-brand invoice or receipt template
**Best for:** ChatGPT (DALL-E)

> A clean, professionally designed invoice template for "[BRAND NAME]". White background with [ACCENT COLOR] used sparingly for the header bar, line item alternating row highlights, and the total amount. Brand logo in the top-left corner. Company details (address, email, phone) in the top-right in small [FONT STYLE] text. Clear sections for: invoice number, date, client details, itemized line items (description, quantity, rate, amount), subtotal, tax, total due, and payment terms. The design is clean, modern, and professional — not a generic spreadsheet. Aspect ratio 3:4 (portrait page).

**Pro tip:** Beautiful invoices get paid faster. Seriously. A professional invoice signals that you run a serious business. Include clear payment terms and your preferred payment method.

### 26. Brand Photography Style Guide
**When to use:** Defining the visual style for brand photography
**Best for:** Midjourney, ChatGPT (DALL-E)

> A brand photography style guide page showing four example photographs in a 2x2 grid. All four images share a cohesive aesthetic: [STYLE — e.g., warm and golden with desaturated greens / cool and minimal with blue undertones / rich and moody with deep shadows]. Image 1: a product shot. Image 2: a lifestyle/people shot. Image 3: a detail/texture close-up. Image 4: an environmental/location shot. Below the grid, text labels reading "DO: [ATTRIBUTES — e.g., natural light, warm tones, authentic moments]" in green and "DON'T: [ANTI-ATTRIBUTES — e.g., flash photography, oversaturated colors, posed smiles]" in red. Clean, design-agency presentation layout. Aspect ratio 4:3.

**Pro tip:** A photography style guide ensures every image your brand produces — whether by you, contractors, or UGC creators — looks like it belongs together.
