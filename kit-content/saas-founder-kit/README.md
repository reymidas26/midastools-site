## Tips for Best Results

### Be Specific with Placeholders
The more detail you provide in the `[bracket]` fields, the better your output will be. Instead of `[product]`, try `[a project management tool for remote engineering teams, starting at $29/mo]`.

### Set Your SaaS Context Once
At the start of each AI session, paste this context block so the AI understands your product:

```
I'm the founder of [PRODUCT NAME], a [PRODUCT CATEGORY] SaaS tool.

Product: [ONE-SENTENCE DESCRIPTION]
Target user: [PRIMARY PERSONA — role, company size, pain point]
Pricing: [FREE TIER?] / [STARTER PRICE] / [PRO PRICE] / [ENTERPRISE?]
Stage: [PRE-LAUNCH / BETA / LAUNCHED / SCALING]
MRR: [CURRENT MRR OR "PRE-REVENUE"]
Key differentiator: [WHAT MAKES YOU DIFFERENT FROM COMPETITORS]
Brand voice: [TONE — e.g., developer-friendly, professional, playful, enterprise-grade]
Website: [URL]
```

After setting context, the AI will tailor every response to your specific SaaS product.

### Iterate and Refine
Your first output might be 80% right. Follow up with requests like:
- "Make it more technical for a developer audience"
- "Shorten this for an in-app tooltip"
- "Add more social proof"
- "Rewrite this for enterprise buyers"

### Batch Your Work
Set aside 2-3 hours per week to generate a full week's worth of emails, marketing copy, and support templates. Batching is far more efficient than creating content on the fly.

### Use Across Your Team
Share customized prompts with your marketing, support, and product teams. These templates scale across your entire organization.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: Do I need a paid AI subscription?**
No. All prompts work with free tiers of ChatGPT, Claude, and Gemini. Paid plans give longer responses and faster speeds, but are not required.

**Q: Can I modify these templates?**
Absolutely. These are yours to customize. Add your brand voice, adjust the tone, and make them your own.

**Q: Are these prompts specific to B2B SaaS or B2C SaaS?**
The majority are written for B2B SaaS, which is the most common model. However, most prompts work for B2C SaaS with minor adjustments to tone and audience placeholders.

**Q: How often should I update my prompts?**
Review and refresh your customized prompts quarterly. As your product evolves, your messaging should too.

**Q: Can I use these for multiple products?**
Yes. Your license covers all products and businesses you personally own and operate.

**Q: What if I get a bad output?**
Add more context, be more specific with placeholders, or ask the AI to "try again with a different approach." The prompts are starting points — iteration is normal.
