## How to Use This Kit

### Step 1: Pick the Right File
Choose the file that matches your presentation type. Each file is organized by specific use case within the category.

### Step 2: Find the Prompt You Need
Each prompt is numbered and includes a description of when to use it. Scan the descriptions to find what matches your situation.

### Step 3: Fill in the [PLACEHOLDERS]
Every prompt has `[PLACEHOLDER]` variables in brackets. Replace these with your specific information:
- `[COMPANY NAME]` — Your company or client name
- `[INDUSTRY]` — Your target industry
- `[AUDIENCE]` — Who will see this presentation
- `[PRODUCT/SERVICE]` — What you're presenting

### Step 4: Paste into Your AI Tool
Copy the entire prompt, fill in the placeholders, and paste it into ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, or your preferred AI. The output is structured content ready for your slides.

### Step 5: Refine and Customize
Use the AI output as your foundation. Adjust tone, add your specific data points, and customize for your audience.

## Pro Tips for Best Results

1. **Be specific with placeholders.** The more detail you provide in the brackets, the better your output. Instead of `[PRODUCT]`, try `[AI-powered CRM for real estate agents]`.

2. **Chain prompts together.** Use the pitch deck prompts in order (1 through 25) to build a complete deck from scratch.

3. **Iterate on outputs.** After your first result, ask the AI to "make the hook more provocative" or "add more specific data points."

4. **Specify your audience.** Adding context like "this is for Series A investors who have seen 50 pitches this week" dramatically improves output quality.

5. **Use the storytelling prompts last.** After generating your content, run it through the slide design & storytelling prompts in file 06 to polish everything.

6. **Request specific formats.** Add "format as bullet points for slides" or "write as speaker notes" to any prompt for different output styles.
