## How Variables Work

Every prompt uses `[BRACKET NOTATION]` for customizable fields:

```
[YOUR BUSINESS] — Your company or brand name
[YOUR INDUSTRY] — Your niche or market
[TARGET AUDIENCE] — Who you're trying to reach
[PRODUCT/SERVICE] — What you sell
[TONE] — casual, professional, witty, authoritative, etc.
```

Replace these with your real information before pasting into your AI tool.

## Pro Tips

1. **Chain prompts together** — Use the output of one prompt as input for another
2. **Save your best outputs** — Build a swipe file of AI-generated content that works
3. **Customize the tone** — Add "Write in a [casual/professional/witty] tone" to any prompt
4. **Ask for variations** — After any output, say "Give me 3 alternative versions"
5. **Add context** — The more specific your [BRACKET] variables, the better the output
6. **Iterate** — First output is a draft. Use follow-up prompts to refine
