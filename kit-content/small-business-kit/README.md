1. **Copy** the prompt template you want to use.

- A Google Doc or Notion page labeled "My AI Prompts"
- A folder on your desktop
- Your project management tool (Trello, Asana, etc.)

## Tips for Best Results

### Be Specific with Placeholders
The more detail you provide in the `[bracket]` fields, the better your output will be. Instead of `[product]`, try `[handmade soy candles in 8oz mason jars, $24 each]`.

### Iterate and Refine
Your first output might be 80% right. Follow up with requests like:
- "Make it more casual"
- "Shorten this to 2 sentences"
- "Add more urgency"
- "Rewrite for a younger audience"

### Set Context Once
At the start of each AI session, paste this context block so the AI understands your business:

```
I run [BUSINESS NAME], a [TYPE OF BUSINESS] based in [LOCATION].
We sell [PRODUCTS/SERVICES] to [TARGET AUDIENCE].
Our brand voice is [TONE: professional, friendly, casual, luxury, etc.].
Our website is [URL].
```

After setting context, the AI will tailor every response to your specific business.

### Use the Calendar Template
The 30-day social media calendar in File 01 is one of the most valuable tools in this kit. Fill it out once per month and you'll never wonder "what should I post today?" again.

### Batch Your Work
Set aside 1-2 hours per week to generate a full week's worth of content, emails, and responses using these templates. Batching is far more efficient than creating content on the fly.

Throughout this kit, you'll see placeholders in `[BRACKETS]`. Here's a quick reference:

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: Do I need a paid AI subscription?**
No. All prompts work with free tiers of ChatGPT, Claude, and Gemini. Paid plans give longer responses and faster speeds, but are not required.

**Q: Can I modify these templates?**
Absolutely. These are yours to customize. Add your brand voice, adjust the tone, and make them your own.

**Q: How often should I update my prompts?**
Review and refresh your customized prompts quarterly. AI tools improve over time, and your business evolves too.

**Q: Can I use these for multiple businesses?**
Yes. Your license covers all businesses you personally own and operate.

**Q: What if I get a bad output?**
Add more context, be more specific with placeholders, or ask the AI to "try again with a different approach." The prompts are starting points — iteration is normal.
