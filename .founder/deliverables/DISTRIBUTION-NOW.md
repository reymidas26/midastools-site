```
The CRAFT framework will 10x your AI prompts:

C — Context (background, situation)
R — Role (who should the AI be?)
A — Action (the specific task)
F — Format (list, table, email, etc.)
T — Tone (formal, casual, bold)

Most people only use A. That's why their results suck.
```
